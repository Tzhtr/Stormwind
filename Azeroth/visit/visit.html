What to do?


Visit the Cathedral of Light

This is the largest and most impressive building in Stormwind, and it serves as the spiritual center of the city. Inside, you can explore the beautiful architecture, light candles in memory of loved ones, and even attend a wedding or other ceremony.

Explore the Park

The park in Stormwind is a beautiful green space with fountains, gardens, and statues. You can take a leisurely stroll, sit on a bench and people-watch, or even try your hand at fishing in the pond.

Go shopping

Stormwind has a bustling marketplace where you can find all sorts of goods, from weapons and armor to food and drink. Be sure to check out the auction house to see if there are any rare items for sale.

Visit the King

If you're feeling bold, you can try to gain an audience with King Varian Wrynn himself. He can be found in his throne room in the Keep, but be warned - getting an appointment with the king is no easy task.

Join a guild

Stormwind is home to many player-run guilds, which are groups of players who work together to complete quests and other activities. Joining a guild is a great way to make friends and experience the game in a more social way.

Challenge!

Try defeating the dungeon Stockade that is located in the heart of the city. It is a level 20-30 dungeon that can be accessed by Alliance players. The Stockade is home to various mobs and bosses that drop valuable loot, making it a popular spot for players looking to level up and gear up.
