Creators of Stormwind


The original developers of World of Warcraft, also known as WoW, were a group of talented designers, programmers, and artists who worked at Blizzard Entertainment. The team was led by Rob Pardo and Jeff Kaplan, who were responsible for overseeing the game's overall design and development. Other notable members of the team included Tom Chilton, who served as the game's lead designer, and Chris Metzen, who helped to create the game's rich lore and storylines. Together, this group of developers created a massively multiplayer online game that captivated millions of players around the world and set the standard for the modern MMORPG genre. Their innovative ideas and dedication to the game's quality helped to make WoW one of the most successful and influential video games of all time.
