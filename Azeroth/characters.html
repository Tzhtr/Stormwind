Notable Characters

Llane Wrynn I


Llane Wrynn I was a character in the Warcraft universe, specifically in the lore of the kingdom of Stormwind. He was the son of King Adamant Wrynn and the father of Varian Wrynn, who would later become the king of Stormwind himself. Llane was a just and fair ruler who was well-liked by his people and was known for his bravery in battle. He was a strong ally of the Alliance and played a key role in the First War against the Orcish Horde. However, he was ultimately betrayed and assassinated by one of his closest advisors, Garona, who was under the control of the Orcish warlock Gul'dan. Llane's death had a profound impact on the people of Stormwind and led to the events that would shape the kingdom's future.

Anduin Lothar


Anduin Lothar was one of the most prominent characters in the Warcraft universe, particularly in the lore of the human kingdom of Stormwind. He was the last descendant of the ancient Arathi bloodline and was known as the Lion of Azeroth. Lothar was a skilled warrior and a master tactician who fought fiercely against the Orcish Horde during the First and Second Wars. He was also a trusted friend and advisor to King Llane Wrynn and played a key role in the formation of the Alliance. Lothar was greatly respected by both his allies and his enemies, and his leadership was instrumental in many of the Alliance's victories. However, he ultimately met his demise in battle against the Horde's Warchief, Orgrim Doomhammer, during the closing days of the Second War. Lothar's death was a devastating loss for the Alliance, and his legacy continued to inspire those who fought for freedom and justice in the years to come.

Varian Wrynn

Varian Wrynn was a key figure in the Warcraft universe, particularly in the lore of the human kingdom of Stormwind. He was the son of King Llane Wrynn and was only a young boy when his father was assassinated during the First War. Varian was forced to flee his homeland and spent many years in exile before eventually returning to claim his rightful place as king of Stormwind. He was a strong and determined leader who fought fiercely against the enemies of the Alliance, including the Orcish Horde and the undead Scourge. Varian was also known for his steadfast devotion to his people and his willingness to do whatever it took to protect them. However, he struggled with personal demons and often struggled to balance his duty as king with his desire to protect his loved ones. Varian ultimately met his demise during the Legion's invasion of Azeroth, sacrificing himself to save his people and his world. His legacy continued to inspire those who fought for freedom and justice in the years to come.

Anduin Wrynn

Anduin Wrynn is a significant character in the Warcraft universe, specifically in the lore of the Alliance. He is the son of Varian Wrynn and was raised to be a compassionate and just leader. Despite his youth, Anduin has already faced numerous challenges in his role as king of Stormwind, including the invasion of the Burning Legion and the ongoing conflict with the Horde. He is known for his pacifistic tendencies and his desire to seek peaceful solutions to conflicts, but he is also willing to fight when necessary to protect his people and his ideals. Anduin's leadership is characterized by his commitment to mercy, compassion, and unity, and he is greatly respected by his allies and his enemies alike. Despite his relative inexperience, Anduin has already shown himself to be a wise and capable leader who has the potential to shape the future of Azeroth for generations to come.
